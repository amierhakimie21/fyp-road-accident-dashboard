Project Background

Road traffic accidents remain a significant public safety concern. This Final Year Project applies data analytics and interactive visualisation techniques to examine accident patterns, severity levels, and contributing factors that influence road safety outcomes.

The project focuses on analytical interpretation of historical data rather than real-time enforcement or operational systems. The aim is to support better understanding of accident trends and risk factors through visual analytics.

Project Aim

The primary aim of this project is to develop an interactive road accident analysis dashboard that enables users to explore accident data effectively. The system supports analytical understanding of trends and risk factors to assist road safety awareness and data-driven decision-making.
