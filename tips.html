Key Road Safety Recommendations

  • Follow Speed Limits – Reduces collision severity and improves reaction time.
  • Avoid Distracted Driving – Mobile phone usage significantly increases accident risk.
  • Be Alert During Peak Hours – Traffic density and fatigue increase accident probability.
  • Use Safety Equipment – Seat belts and helmets reduce injury severity.
  • Drive Carefully in Poor Conditions – Rain and low visibility require extra caution.

Awareness Message

By understanding accident patterns through data analysis, road users can adopt safer driving practices. Increased awareness and responsible behaviour play a critical role in reducing accident risks and saving lives.
